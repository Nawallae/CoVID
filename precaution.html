Precautionary Measures to Take


Citizens
  • Wash hands before/after eating, after touching common public items (eg. door handles, transaction machines, bus handles)
  • Do not touch face
  • Cover mouth when coughing with elbow
  • Cover sneeze with elbow
  • Do not stand or talk too close to others. Stand at least 100cm apart
  • If you start to show symptoms of COVID-19 immediately self isolate for 14 days and contact emergency services

Health Care Workers
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Wear gloves and protective face masks when dealing with patients
  • After dealing with patients, wash hands and disinfect all tools, bedding and equipment used
  • Ensure there is sufficient air circulation
  • Keep at least 200cm distance between patients and visitors/other patients
  • Advice all infected individuals to remain in self isolation for 14 days
  • Advice all individuals who were in contact with infected persons to self isolate for 14 days
  • immediately document all cases of infected persons as well as who they have been in contact with
