Emergency and health care workers are continuing to work together to collect data on who is infected with CoVID-19 and provide them with the care they need. Any updates will be posted as soon as they becoem available.

What you should do

Please continue to take precaution to protect yourself and your community from CoVID-19. In addition to this please refrain from:

  • Smoking
  • Wearing multiple face masks
  • touching face, especially when outside of your home
